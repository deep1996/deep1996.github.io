Our Vision

To produce qualitative and energetic teachers for playing their significant role in the emerging knowledgeable society for educational reconstruction tending to result in total reconstruction of the nation by adhering to statement from prospectus. To play this role effectively we at Shyamlal Saraswati Mahavidyalaya, Saraswati Vihar, Shikarpur, Bulandshahr, (U.P.), strive to equip students with the appropriate knowledge, habits, attitudes and values.

Our Values

  1. Education to all, willing to become teacher Educators, irrespective of caste, creed or religion.
  2. To develop the spirit of fellowship among students and to prepare them to the global citizens through co-operation and peaceful co-existence.
  3. To inspire students for life long learning.

Our Objectives

In order to fulfill this mission, the college helps the prospective teachers to

  1. Acquire academic and professional competencies.
  2. Create awareness and encourage the teacher trainees for their active participation in extra-curricular activities as well as in the organization of seminars, workshop and Research related programmes for their total development.
  3. Develop effective communication skill and leadership qualities.
  4. Motivate and stimulate the teacher-trainees for their active participation in the teaching-learning process.
  5. Give the pupil teachers an understanding of basic principles of education.
  6. Train pupil teachers in various educational techniques and management of school and college.
  7. Acquaint them generally with the social, cultural & educational framework of education.
  8. Provide quality knowledge of information technology applicable to school and college.
  9. Help them to avail job opportunities and facilities of self employment for the young generation.
