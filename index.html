Our Mission

We aim to provide qualitative education to the pupil teachers, to improve their teaching skills and development of their competencies necessary to play the versatile role of the teacher in the new millennium, so that the future generation and pillars of the nation may get efficient teachers to teach them. "Vidhya Bharti All India Educational Institution" is a well known name in the field of education. Main aim of "Vidhya Bharti" is the whole development of youth so that they can serve their country best. Fortunately " Shyamlal Saraswati Mahavidyalaya, Shikarpur " affiliated to Vidhya Bharti is one of the name of these efforts. It was the demand of society from many years in this rural area that there should ne ab institution which can provide cultuted higher education and prove itself the mile stone in the field of education. So it was planned to establish a degree college with the help of the representatives of the society in the peaceful atmosphere of Saraswati Vihar. Shikarpur which is situated only 20 km. away from Bulandshahr on Meerut-Badaun Highway.Saraswati Shiksha Samiti, shikarpur (Society) was registered under the society Registration Act 1860. The Foundation of Shyamlal Saraswati Mahavidyalaya took place on 16 Dec. 1994 through the hands of Hornable Vajpai Ji. Shyamlal Saraswati Mahavidyalaya, Saraswati Vihar, Shikarpur, District- Bulandshahr, (U.P.), governed by (Regd. body) Saraswati Shiksha Samiti Shikarpur, District-Bulandshahr is one of the premier institute imparting B.Ed. education in Shikarpur, District Bulandshahr (U.P.). This college boasts of highly qualified and one of the best faculty imparting quality and value education to its students. The college has ultra modern facilities to strike a right balance between academic and over all personality development of pupil teachers by having lots of Co-curricular activities going round the session. The management of the college works keeping in mind his vision, mission, values and Objectives.
